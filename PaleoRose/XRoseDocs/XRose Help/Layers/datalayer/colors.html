Depending on the type of plot, both the line colors and fill colors may be set.

To change either line or fill color,

1. Open the inspector panel (click on the toolbar icon).

2. Choose the data layer in the layers table.

3. Select "Appearence" in the uppermost popup button.

4. Click on the line or fill color well and change the color.

To change both line and fill color at the same time,

1. Do steps 1-3 above.

2. Click on the line color well.

3. Shift-click on the fill color well.

4. Proceed with changing colors.
