Core Layer

XRose allows the use to "hide" the circle lines from view.

To change visibility,

1. Open the inspector panel (click on the toolbar icon).

2. Click on the grid layer in the layers table.

3. Select "Circles" in the uppermost popup button.

4. Click on the "Visibile Circles" switch.
Note: if both radials and circles are visible, the layer will be invisible even if the layer visibility shows "visible" in the layers table.
