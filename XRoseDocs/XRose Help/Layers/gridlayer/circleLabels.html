Circle Labels
Showing and Customizing Labels

Core Layer

Unlike radial labels, circle labels are relatively simple. You can set their font, their position, and their visibility. The label's position is defined by a circular slider representing their position on the circle. The labels are then aligned parallel to a line tangent to the circle at the set angle.

Working with circle labels,

1. Open the inspector panel (click on the toolbar icon).

2. Click on the grid layer in the layers table.

3. Select "Circle Labels" in the uppermost popup button.

4. Adjust the label controls as desired.

Note: Setting the labels to a postion below the East-West line will show the labels as inverted. This may not be desirable.
