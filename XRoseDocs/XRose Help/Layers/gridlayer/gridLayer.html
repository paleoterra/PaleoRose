Core Layer

The grid layer is the only layer added to the diagram by default. It has two basic elements: rings, and radials. Rings are the concentric circles and the radials are the lines radiating from the center of the diagram. The frequence and apperence of these lines are user editable. The grid system also supports a basic labeling system.
